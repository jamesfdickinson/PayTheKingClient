How to Play

Objective

Be the last one standing

You start with 100 gold. Each round you need to pay the king. Whoever pays the king the least is out. Rounds continue until one player remains. The last player standing wins!

Note: If you use up all your gold, you will not be able to pay the king and will lose.

Scoring

If you win, you gain points for the remaining gold you did not use.

If you lose, you lose 1 point.
